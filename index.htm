Xiaochen LI

李晓晨

[中文]

Xiaochen LI is a Ph.D. candidate at OSCAR Lab, in Dalian University of Technology, China, under supervision with Prof. He Jiang from 2015.

Before that, he worked for the Bechelor of Software Engineering at School of Software in Dalian University of Technology from 2008, continued with the Master of Software Engineering at School of Software from 2012. Before university, he was graduated from Harbin No. 3 High School, Harbin, China.

His research interests include mining software repositories, open-source software engineering, and software semantic analysis.
